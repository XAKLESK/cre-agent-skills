# Industrial v1 Research Map

This directory contains the supporting research notes for the first industrial expansion of `cre-agent-skills`.

## Purpose

- Provide source-backed support for new industrial skills and knowledge bases
- Keep benchmarks, assumptions, and market-specific reasoning traceable
- Separate research detail from the prompt layer so skills stay concise and usable

## Industrial v1 Scope

- Geography: United States only
- Workflow scope: acquisitions core
- Property focus: industrial and logistics, including bulk distribution, shallow-bay, flex industrial, light industrial, and select single-tenant / multi-tenant situations

## Cross-Note Standards

Each note should include:

- Purpose and supported file(s)
- U.S.-only assumptions
- Dated source table
- Benchmark and formula decisions
- Conflicting-source resolution
- Edge cases and red flags
- Open questions

## Current Source Themes

The initial Industrial v1 research set draws primarily from:

- NAIOP industrial research and terminology resources
- JLL, CBRE, and Cushman & Wakefield industrial market publications
- U.S. Census and Bureau of Transportation Statistics releases
- SBA program guidance
- Institutional lender materials from MetLife, PGIM, and related debt-market sources
- SEC filings and operating disclosures from industrial REITs such as Prologis, STAG, Rexford, and EastGroup
