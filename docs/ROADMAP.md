# Roadmap

This roadmap reflects the intended direction of `cre-agent-skills` as a broader U.S. commercial real estate skills library.

## Current State

- Multifamily acquisitions toolkit
- Shared CRE legal, closing, and document-ingestion tools
- Research-backed Industrial v1 expansion

## Next Planned Waves

### 1. Multifamily Depth

Focus areas:

- Asset management workflows
- Lease-up and concessions analysis
- Value-add execution tracking
- Renewal, collections, and delinquency review
- Affordable and workforce housing overlays

### 2. Office

Focus areas:

- Office lease abstraction
- TI/LC heavy underwriting
- Occupancy-cost and rollover analysis
- Flight-to-quality and submarket competition review
- Lender-fit guidance for office in a selective capital environment

### 3. Retail / Self-Storage

Focus areas:

- Retail center leasing and tenant-mix risk
- Co-tenancy, exclusives, and anchor analysis
- Self-storage operating metrics and revenue management
- Property-type-specific underwriting and lender criteria

## Guiding Principles

- Stay markdown-first
- Keep sector-specific research visible
- Preserve stable existing paths when possible
- Prefer reusable knowledge bases over duplicative prompt logic
